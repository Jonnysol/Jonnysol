# Jonathan Solomon

I build practical software experiments around AI assistants, media tools, community infrastructure, and voice-first interfaces.

## Featured Work

- [Movieport](https://github.com/Jonnysol/Movieport) - voice-controlled streaming TV experience with local Whisper and smart media matching.
- [TimeCapsuleTV](https://github.com/Jonnysol/TimeCapsuleTV) - retro TV kiosk for browsing curated video libraries through a decade-based interface.
- [StreamGrid](https://github.com/Jonnysol/StreamGrid) - infinite-canvas video grid for monitoring live streams and m3u8 feeds.
- [NASA-Based-Plant-Prediction-Software](https://github.com/Jonnysol/NASA-Based-Plant-Prediction-Software) - plant prediction project with crop pages, solar calculations, docs, and tests.
- [DCESK8](https://github.com/Jonnysol/DCESK8) - Washington DC electric skateboarding and personal electric vehicle community site.
- [Amharic AI Voice Chatbot](https://github.com/Jonnysol/Amharic-AI-Voice-Chatbot) - Amharic voice chatbot project for Raspberry Pi.

## Areas I Keep Coming Back To

- Desktop agents and voice-controlled workflows
- Video, streaming, and media discovery tools
- Community sites and maps for real-world groups
- Amharic language technology and accessible voice interfaces
- Fast prototypes that turn research ideas into runnable demos
